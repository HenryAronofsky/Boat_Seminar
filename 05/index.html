### Week 5: Forming the Hull Plane down excess epoxy on butt joints. Cut temporary spreaders. Form the plywood pieces into the shape of the hull. #### Team speed docs This week the boat finally started to take form and though we had some issues, it ended up working out. We then cut our temporary spreaders out of scrap, according to the lengths provided by Nathan. We then started to assemble, drilling the sides of our boat into the spreaders. The boat started to take shape but it was clear that something was wrong with our curvature as the hull of our boat did not fit into the outline of the sides at all.
